## What is NLP
- field of linguistics and machine learning
- enables computers to understand and communicate with human language

## Common NLP tasks
- Classifying whole sentences
- Sentiment Analysis
- Generating text content
- Extracting an answer from a text
- Speech Recognition

## Few Common Applications
- Virtual Assistants
- Translation
- Sentiment Analysis
- Grammar checkers

## LLMs

An AI model trained on massive amounts of text data that can understand and generate human-like text, recognize patterns in language, and perform a wide variety of language tasks without task-specific training.
